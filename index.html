About us

Founded in November 2023 under the name "RoboFIIR" and evolving into sySTEMatic Robotics, we are a team of over 70 students from the Faculty of Industrial Engineering and Robotics at the National University of Science and Technology Politehnica Bucharest. United by a passion for innovation, our technical and non-technical members collaborate to design, build, and program fully integrated robots from mechanical architecture and embedded systems to autonomous control and real-time strategy software for applied research and competitive engineering on international stages, including Greece, China, and Japan. Whether competing in Sumo, Line Follower, or Freestyle, our mission is to place Romania on the global robotics map through engineering excellence and team spirit. We thrive on real-world challenges that push us out of our comfort zone, test our ideas, and force us to adapt to any situation.

Our goals

● Develop innovative, student-led robotics projects

● Encourage hands-on learning and technical growth

● Promote teamwork across engineering, programming, and design

● Foster creativity, collaboration, and problem-solving

● Prepare members for future careers in technology and engineering

Competitions

Our creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving skills are put to the test in every project we take on.

Challenges are tackled enthusiastically, pushing us to innovate, work efficiently, and turn bold ideas into real achievements.

ROBOTCHALLENGE

– China

Participating in this competition was an extraordinarily beautiful experience and a lesson in cultural and technical openness. Beyond the pride of traveling with our robots to a completely different country, the most fascinating aspect was the opportunity to directly observe the Asian style of play across all competition events. We were amazed to see completely new approaches compared to what we usually encountered at competitions in Europe. This diversity of perspectives showed us how important it is to experience robotics on a global scale.

ROBOCHALLENGE

– Bucharest, Romania

Robochallenge was an action-packed experience. Although the atmosphere was chaotic at times, the energy of the place kept us on our toes. The most important aspect of this challenge was not just managing the unpredictable, but the profound sense of belonging. Feeling the thrill of representing our team and country in a competition of such magnitude was an absolutely memorable experience.

MINOAN ROBOTSPORTS COMPETITION

– Greece

Minoan Robotsports Competition was a perfect mix of adrenaline, challenges, and collaboration. We explored less well-known competition categories, participating in unique events such as combat, drones, and football. It was a fantastic opportunity to push our limits, and even more than the trophies we won, we took away a deep gratitude for our mentor and the joy of meeting amazing people from all over the world.
